# Code of Conduct

This project aims to be a respectful, constructive place to collaborate.

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful in disagreements
- assume good intent
- give actionable technical feedback
- avoid harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or access that harm the project or its contributors.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. Use the hosting platform's abuse or moderation reporting tools
2. Keep sensitive personal information out of public reports

Maintainers will review reports in good faith, protect reporter privacy when possible, and take whatever moderation action is appropriate for the safety of the project.
